this is a help Page.

in this page is explained how you play the game and what all the options are

camera movement:

  • you move the camera with the buttons A,W,D and S
  • you zoom-in by holding the spacebar and zoom-out by releasing the spacebar.

character movement:

  • hover the mouse over the character you want to move
  • hold click and drag the movementcircle of the player toward to location you want the player to be
  • press start to let all character move to their planned locations
